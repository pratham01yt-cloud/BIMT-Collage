Dean (Academics), (BIMT, Badaun)

As the Dean (Academics) of BIMT, it is my privilege to guide and support the academic growth of our students. We are dedicated to maintaining high standards of education while fostering innovation, critical thinking, and practical knowledge among learners.

At BIMT, we emphasize not only theoretical learning but also skill development, research, and overall personality enhancement. Our aim is to create a strong academic foundation that empowers students to achieve success in their careers and contribute positively to society.
